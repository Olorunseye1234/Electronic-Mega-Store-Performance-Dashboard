# Electronic-Mega-Store-Performance-Dashboard
This dashboard presents a comprehensive EDA-driven view of Electronic Mega Store’s sales performance. It summarizes total sales and margins, reveals trends over time, highlights top product categories and channels, and surfaces geographic patterns by zones and states. It also highlights the impact of promotion and identifies high- and low-performing production.
